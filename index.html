Understanding Burnout

Burnout is a state of emotional, physical, and mental exhaustion caused by excessive and prolonged stress. Learn about the signs and symptoms to identify it early.

Causes of Burnout

Explore common causes of burnout, such as workplace stress, lack of control, and poor work-life balance. Identifying these can help in prevention.

Symptoms of Burnout

Discover the physical and emotional symptoms of burnout, including fatigue, irritability, and difficulty concentrating, to better understand your state.

Prevention Strategies

Learn effective strategies to prevent burnout, such as setting boundaries, practicing mindfulness, and ensuring adequate rest and self-care.

Recovery and Support

Find out how to recover from burnout with professional support, lifestyle changes, and coping mechanisms to regain balance and improve well-being.
