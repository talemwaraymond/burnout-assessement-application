Celebrating the World Mental Health Day

The workplace environment has a significant impact on employees' mental health, and addressing mental health concerns in professional settings is more crucial than ever. In an increasingly fast-paced world, work-related stress, anxiety, and burnout are becoming common, with substantial consequences on productivity, employee well-being, and organizational growth.

As we celebrate the upcoming World Mental Health Day, the event is themed around prioritizing mental health in the workplace. It will serve as a platform for discussing the importance of mental health awareness in professional settings. The event will focus on how employers and employees can contribute to building healthier, more supportive workplace environments.

The goal is to create an interactive and educational experience for participants to learn the significance of mental health in the workplace, provide practical tools for stress management, and promote a culture of well-being.

Date and Time

Tuesday, 8 Oct 2024, 08:00 AM - 01:00 PM EAT

Location

National ICT Innovation Hub, Nakawa

Agenda

  • Welcome from your host: Innerspark will introduce the importance of workplace mental health.
  • Panel Discussion: Insights from mental health experts and HR leaders on workplace mental health.
  • Group and Individual sessions: Personalized sessions to address specific concerns.
  • Wrap-up & Networking: Ongoing mental health programs and networking opportunities.
